8th Grade

Erick ended his 8th Grade year with a 3.8 GPA. It was one of his most challengeing years yet to come, and has to be his 2nd hardest school year. Before highschool, he had gone to a small Catholic school called St. William School & Parish. He went to this school his whole life, from Pre-k to 8th grade. In this time he learn and concepted ideas he still uses till this day and was welcomed everyday with a warm hello and smile. Today, St. William remains a strong root of his life, from the regular conversations he has, to the representaton he shows to others. He encourgaes parents to attend their kids at St. William, for its strongly kind and welcoming environment.

9th Grade/Freshman Year

Erick's Freshman year had to be his hardest yet. This was the year he had to make a whole new set of years, create a new and exhausting schedule, and of course pass all of his newly difficult classes. Yet dispises all the challenges, Erick is still well and striving. He found a new group of welcoming kind friends, he quickly adapted to the new schedule and even joined several sports. He even ended his first sesmester of highschool with a 4.4 GPA! He can play his positions of Football well, he is a good Heavyweight in wrestling, and most of all he is a academic weapon. Though all of these accomplishments didn't come easily. Erick worked tirlessly, with little to no solid time for his homework, sports taking up majority of his after school time and free time, and having difficulty fitting into his new school. But through all the challenges, it eventually worked out for him in the end.
